Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need coding experience to apply?

No prior experience is required. We accept learners from all backgrounds. What matters most is your motivation, logical thinking, and willingness to work hard.

Is the programme really free?

Yes. Grit Lab Africa charges no tuition fees. The programme is fully funded by our partners and supporters. You may be required to access a reliable internet connection and computer.

Is the programme online or in-person?

We offer both on-campus (Accra, Lagos, Nairobi) and remote tracks. You can specify your preference in your application.
