Our Journey

How We Started

From a single room in Accra to a pan-African movement ‚Äî here's how GLA came to be.

2019

Founded in Accra, Ghana

Grit Lab Africa was founded by a group of passionate technologists who saw first-hand the talent gap across the continent and decided to act.

2020

First Cohort Graduates

Our inaugural cohort of 24 learners graduated with a 100% project completion rate and 85% job placement within 3 months.

2021

Expansion to Lagos & Nairobi

GLA opened its second and third campuses in Nigeria and Kenya, tripling our annual training capacity.

2022

Women in Tech Initiative

We launched a dedicated programme to increase female representation in our cohorts, reaching 42% women enrollment.

2023

African EdTech Award

GLA was recognised as Africa's most impactful edtech organisation by the Pan-African Digital Skills Council.

2025

500+ Graduates & Counting

We celebrated our 500th graduate, with alumni now working at leading tech companies across 8 African countries and around the world.
