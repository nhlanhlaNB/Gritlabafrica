About GRIT Lab Africa

Our Mission

GRIT Lab Africa is a transformative innovation and skills development initiative dedicated to empowering young Africans with practical programming expertise, entrepreneurial mindset, and the grit required to build meaningful technology solutions.

Founded & Leadership

Started by Prof. Abejide Ade-Ibijola, GRIT Lab Africa offers free training in computer programming skills and provides mentoring in mindset, discipline, and GRIT to young people throughout Africa.

Training Programmes

Six comprehensive programmes covering problem-solving, web development, mobile app development, 3D/VR/AR, Artificial Intelligence, and digital technologies for small businesses across multiple African countries.

Impact & Recognition

3000+ trained since inception, 100% graduate employment rate, 80+ technological tools developed, and multiple prestigious awards including African Tech Innovation Award and African Innovation Hub of the Year.
